Batching versus Grouping Messages

Batching refers to the process of combining individual messages into a single message, whereas grouping refers to the process of designating a set of individual messages as a group.

Grouping is used in the context of Message Collectors. Collectors generally do not batch messages, but instead group messages and release them at the same time as individual messages. Grouped messages are rendered in the Message View page in the Management Console as individual messages, as opposed to batched messages, which are displayed as a single message.
