Implementation Models

The two primary models of implementation of Rhapsody are:

  • Hub-and-Spoke Model
  • SOA Healthcare Bus

Hub-and-Spoke Model

The Hub-and-Spoke model, named after a bicycle wheel, has a central hub with a series of one-to-many connections through the integration environment. Almost all traffic (messages) is routed between destinations and the hub, with little to no direct connectivity between destinations.

SOA Healthcare Bus

The SOA Healthcare Bus model is based on the enterprise service bus and is designed to distribute information across an enterprise quickly and easily, by masking differences among underlying platforms, architectures and network protocols.
