Installation Caveats

Before you install Rhapsody, ensure you review the information in this section and determine whether it is applicable to your configuration:

Upgrade Caveat

Description
Missing fontconfig library Due to the adoption of OpenAdoptJDK in Rhapsody 6.5.0, the fontconfig library should be pre-installed on non-GUI 64-bit *nix platforms before running the Rhapsody engine installer. Installations on other platforms are not affected.
