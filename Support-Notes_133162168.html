Support Notes

The Support Notes tab enables you to attach free-form Rich Text Format support notes to a communication point. These notes are displayed on the Communication Points Details page in the Management Console and included in any generated documentation. The text can be formatted using the formatting tool bar and is limited to 2500 characters.
