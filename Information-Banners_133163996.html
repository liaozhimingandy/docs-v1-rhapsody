Information Banners

Information banners may be displayed at the top of the Management Console pages to alert the user to any one of the following situations:

When... Refer to...
Data Validation is in progress Data Validation Service
Disk space is low Low Disk Space Warning
In archive mode (a backup is being restored) Forensic Restore
All notifications are suspended Suspend / Resume All Notifications
Backups have not been configured for certain backup areas Backup and Restore
Heap dump files are generated after the JVM runs out of memory Heap Dump Files Warning
