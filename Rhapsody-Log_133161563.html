Rhapsody Log

Rhapsody maintains a log which keeps a precise record of what it has been doing and what errors have occurred while processing messages. The Rhapsody log is the first resource to use when troubleshooting, as often it contains details or error messages describing the problem and what Rhapsody is doing about it:
