Modifying a Condition

To modify an existing condition:

  1. Select a conditional connector on your route, and click the Edit link of the condition you want to modify.

  2. Modify the condition as required. Refer to Adding a New Condition for field definitions.

    If you have more than one condition, you can reorder the conditions by dragging and dropping them.
