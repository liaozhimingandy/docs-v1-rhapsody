Support Notes (Filters)

The Support Notes tab enables you to attach free-form Rich Text Format notes to a filter. These notes are displayed on the Filter Details page in the Management Console, and included in any generated documentation. Support Notes can help you to identify and document the purpose of the filter. The text can be formatted using the formatting toolbar and is limited to 2500 characters.
